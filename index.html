About Tirupati Consulting Group

Tirupati Consulting Group (TCG) is envisioned to empower MSMEs across a wide spectrum of domains—to overcome growth challenges. We provide tailored support in business development, lead generation, and bid submission.

Our expertise spans multiple infrastructure and service domains, and we leverage our deep industry network to generate qualified leads and guide our MSME clients through winning bids. Once a purchase order is secured, our clients take the reins, delivering their projects with confidence and efficiency.
